## COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE BANKING: KEY PAIN POINTS EXPLAINED

### **RATE COMPRESSION**
- **What it is**: Loan spreads declining (285 → 245bps in 18 months)
- **Why it matters**: Direct impact to revenue. 40bps compression on $1.8B portfolio = $7.2M annual revenue loss
- **The trap**: Can't lower rates to stay competitive without destroying ROA
- **The solution**: Speed becomes the differentiator. "We can't compete on rate, but we can on cycle time"

### **TIME-TO-CLOSE DELAYS**
- **Current state**: 45-60 days for underwriting + approval
- **Competitor state**: JPMorgan 28-32 days, BREIT 25-30 days
- **Impact**: Lost 12+ deals in 2025 ($180M pipeline lost)
- **Root cause**: Manual underwriting, spreadsheet-based workflows, inefficient document collection
- **The math**: If you close in 25 days instead of 50, you can deploy capital faster = higher portfolio yield

### **UNDERWRITING ACCURACY**
- **Current problem**: 8% exception rate (1-2 exceptions per month)
- **Cost per exception**: $45K in rework (staff time, re-runs, compliance reviews)
- **Annual impact**: $45-90K monthly = $540K-$1.08M annually
- **Compliance risk**: 2 FIRREA violations last year = regulatory scrutiny

### **CAPITAL DEPLOYMENT TIMING**
- **Current state**: 14-18 days post-close before capital actually deployed
- **Opportunity cost**: Capital earning 0% during settlement period
- **Annual impact**: $420M portfolio × 16 days = $180K+ in lost deployment income
- **Solution**: Streamlined closing = faster deployment = capital earning spread sooner

### **LOAN OFFICER RETENTION**
- **The issue**: Senior LOs leaving because volume is too low (8 deals/year when they could do 12)
- **Root cause**: Slow underwriting = fewer closes = lower compensation
- **Replacement cost**: $200K+ per LO (recruiting + 6-month ramp)
- **The fix**: Speed increases LO productivity = higher deal volume = better comp = retention
